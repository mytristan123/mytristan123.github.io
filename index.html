The world of Tristan Maiocchi



1. Hobbies

I never find enough time to do everything I want, but I particularly like going to the cinema, the theater, a concert, visiting an exhibition or a monument, strolling without a goal necessarily precise.

2. Movies

I love watching movies, and most of my favorite movies are action movies or thrillers. Amongst this, my favorite directors are Steven Spielberg, I have seen almost all his films.

3. Cars

This passion has been passed on to me by my father since my early childhood. Indeed, professional mechanic, my father has always had beautiful cars. Since small, I already aspired to drive. Today, I love to go rallying to watch beautiful cars and see car races.

4. Videos games

This is for me one of the best distractions that can exist. I feel really good when I play, it's a way to let off steam. My favorites are FPS games and Sports games like Call of Duty or FIFA.

5. Sport

I have always loved the sport even if for some time, I really feel the need. I like sport for many different things, the sport allows you to clear your head, to think about nothing, to spend, to evacuate his bad mood ... When I play sports I feel well in tune with myself and I love it. I love to feel the effort it takes and the pleasure when you succeed. I love sport because it's a learning experience, sometimes you lose, sometimes you win, but you always learn about your losses and that's what I like.
